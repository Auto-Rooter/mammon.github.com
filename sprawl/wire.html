_____Where is the old stuff?

There's a directory called 'old' with the old page in it. Use that for external links, or for {gasp} content. Internal links will not work.

Note that for the moment I am not offering any binary files. Keeping up multiple freebie sites with text and file caches gets to be tedious when one does not have the opportunity to update them and keep them open. Rather than suffer through the many 'bad link' emails, I will instead restrict my page to smaller, text-based content. Note that many 'workspace' and 'filebase' links will be broken because of this; I may in the future fix them. Exceptions to this rule are found in the 'Lost' directory.

The current site has the following directories: Lost, Old, Text, idc, sprawl, tales. Lost contains legacy win32 apps that are referred to in the Tales, but which I will most likely never use again. Old contains a copy of the old site; I have renamed the index page to 'red.html' so that the entire dir can be browsed. Text contains a number of non-Tales texts I have written, or have enjoyed reading. IDC contains my old idc scripts, sprawl contains the html of the new site, and tales is a sub-page dedicated to --get this-- the Tales.

One final note: one of the practices of the new site is to up unfinished text documents. Feel free to read the but keeo in mind that they are unfinished. Errors should be reported; everything else can wait for the final release.
